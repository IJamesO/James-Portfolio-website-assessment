Problem-Solving Page – Design Concept

The Problem-Solving Page was designed to simulate a realistic digital transformation solution for a restaurant business. Its structure guides the user through a clear journey, beginning with the business context, followed by a visually engaging menu display, and concluding with an interactive reservation form.

1. Purpose & Structure

The layout mirrors modern restaurant websites by presenting context, services, and interactive tools in a logical order. The menu section uses visual card layouts to resemble digital ordering systems, while the booking form represents a functional reservation interface.

2. Visual & UX Design Approach

Consistency, readability, and accessibility guided the design theme. A dark background combined with bright highlight colours improves contrast and visual focus. All components maintain spacing and styling consistent with the rest of the portfolio for a unified experience.

3. Interactive Components

Hover effects, image-based menu cards, scroll reveal animations, and form validation demonstrate practical user-experience features. These mimic real digital platforms and reinforce the concept of interactive transformation in hospitality.

4. Real-World Applicability

The page models how restaurants use digital tools to improve customer engagement, highlight products, and streamline operations. By integrating dynamic visuals and functional components, the design shows an understanding of how technology enhances business processes.

5. Why This Design Works

The combination of structured content, strong UX principles, and interactive behaviour results in a professional digital solution. It aligns with industry standards and satisfies the brief’s emphasis on innovation, technology usage, and user-focused design.
